Typographic Poster:
Touching The Void

For my Typography II class, I created a poster and motion clip for the Museum of Modern Art’s exhibition Touching the Void. The design pairs a precise grid with pixelated, high-contrast typography, transforming type into image to explore texture and rhythm. Two accompanying black-and-white studies extend this exploration through repetition and distortion, reflecting the exhibition’s focus on form, structure, and perception
