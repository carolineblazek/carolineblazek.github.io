Katonah Wine & Liquor

For Katonah Wine & Liquor, I designed a series of web graphics, product banners, and event promotions that refreshed the brand’s digital presence and encouraged audience engagement. I also created print and digital newsletters that featured seasonal imagery and thoughtful layout design, helping the brand increase subscriber interaction and maintain a cohesive look across all platforms.

Washington University: Poker Club

As Head of Marketing for WashU Poker Club, I developed a unified visual identity that gave the organization a more professional and recognizable presence across campus. I designed the club’s logo, selected typefaces, and created event branding and promotional materials for tournaments and social media. These campaigns helped build a consistent look and tone, driving new engagement and fostering a sense of community.
